Places Must Visit

Phi Phi Island

I had visited Phi Phi Island on the 1st of July, 2023

Phi Phi Island was a great experience because of the view, it really depends on luck if you will enjoy your trip or not because in Thailand the chance or rain is very likely and random. If its raining it will be a bouncy ride to get there and you wont have as much fun as you would if it wasn't raining, so check the weather before you go.

Bangkok floating market

I had visited Bangkok floating market on the 6th of July, 2023

Bangkok floating market has it all, it have so many cool things makde out of wood and cloth but most of the things are overpriced by a lot so you might have to bargain, but don't worry, they still agree most of the times.
